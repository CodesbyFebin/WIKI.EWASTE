**NIST SP 800-88** (Guidelines for Media Sanitization) is the U.S. government standard for secure data destruction. Published by the National Institute of Standards and Technology, NIST 800-88 defines three levels of data removal:
- **Clear**: Logical deletion
- **Purge**: Overwriting or cryptographic erasure
- **Destroy**: Physical destruction

**In India**: DPDP Act 2023 references NIST 800-88 as a compliant method for secure data deletion.
