#### E-Waste Categories (300 words)
- Computing equipment
- Mobile devices
- Batteries & accumulators
- Consumer electronics
- Medical/industrial equipment
- Includes images & diagrams

#### Recycling Process (500 words)
- Collection
- Transportation & storage
- Segregation & disassembly
- Material recovery
- Final disposal
- Link to facility pages

#### Government Regulations (400 words)
- E-Waste Rules 2022/2023
- KSPCB requirements
- DPDP Act 2023
- Compliance checkboxes
- Link to compliance hub

#### FAQs (300 words)
- 8-10 common questions
- JSON-LD schema markup
- Links to related articles

#### Related Services (150 words)
- ITAD link
- Data destruction link
- Pickup service link
- Compliance audit link

#### Sources & References (150 words)
- CPCB official guidelines
- KSPCB documentation
- Academic research
- Government notifications

**Schema Markup:**
- Article schema (headline, body, author, date)
